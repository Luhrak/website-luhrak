About

Since I childhood I have been interested in drawing. In 2017, I started with digital art for the first time and began a Youtube channel with the name Luhrak later that year. Over the time my online persona developed, I practiced drawing digitally and posting it online.

I have switched my focus from animation to streams to speedarts. A lot of those projects have stopped but doing art and creative projects will always be a thing for me. This site, my Telegram channel and Discord server are the best ways to keep up with what i do.

What do you use to draw?

While I do believe that art can be done well with pretty much any medium and tools, this is still a common question.

So this is what I normally use:

  • Drawing tablet - Wacom Intuos Pro M
  • Art Software - Clip Studio Paint V2
  • Reference Viewer - PureRef

Luhrak the character

My fursona is a 5-foot orange furred finnish spitz. He got darker orange markings on his ears between his eyes and his limbs as well as a creme colored belly, tail underside and arm stripes. He is a self insert meaning his personality is the same as my own.

Making fanart of him is definitely allowed and much appreciated. Feel free to also send it to me or on me discord in the fanart channel.
