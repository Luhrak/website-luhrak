Raimond

This is my harengon phantom rogue named Raimond. Made him for a DND campaign that I joined.

Basically he shared a history with one of the groups' character in Neverwinter and then it fell to a dragon. He always seemed a bit more lucky than his friend but never did he think this Luck actually stems from Tymora - the goddess of luck herself. She brought him back and send to help the adventure group. Yes hes got all the luck he could want but does that mean all his skills and deeds are just because a god happened to help out? What is he even capable of himself?
