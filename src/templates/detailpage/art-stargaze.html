Comfy Stargaze

Funnily enough this art started as a doodle in my uni notes, desperately trying not to fall asleep in a lecture. Then I sat down at home and done a proper drawing of this motive. It has been really nice to just make a comfy art for just myself, no commission, no pressure. I think it is important to sometimes just go back to drawing how you feel like, just like when I started getting into drawing.
