Making my own fursuit

Ever since my first furry convention - that being Eurofurence 27 in 2023 - I have grown more and more of a desire to have a fursuit of my own character. It took me another convention with Nordic Fuzz Con in early 2024 for me to actually come up with a plan.

The plan consisted of me making my own for both financial and creative reasons. I could save some money while also having full control over how the result is gonna look like. As an artist it really can not be that hard right? Well...

I video documented my whole process of making my suit. It is a lot of footage and I edit it down greatly to hopefully have it a fun watch while also being informative. ( Part 2 will still follow once I edit it! )

Part 1 on YouTube 🡵

Now I have my suit and already went to quite a few conventions, walks, meets and dances. Im so happy with it and it was definitely worth it for me.

And best part is that I can make cool pictures now, be it on cons or just anywhere I travel with the suit. And who knows maybe even more cool projects follow it. I would love to join some panels on conventions or do some dance related stuff.
